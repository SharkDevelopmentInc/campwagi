About Us

Camp Wagi is a summer camp for kids and teens who love the outdoors. We offer a variety of programs and activities that are designed to help campers build self-confidence, develop new skills, and make lifelong memories.

Programs

We offer a range of programs to suit the interests and abilities of every camper. From outdoor adventure and sports to arts and crafts and science, there's something for everyone.

Activities

Our daily schedule is packed with fun and exciting activities, including:

  • Hiking and backpacking
  • Canoeing and kayaking
  • Swimming and diving
  • Arts and crafts
  • Sports and games
  • Campfires and sing-alongs
